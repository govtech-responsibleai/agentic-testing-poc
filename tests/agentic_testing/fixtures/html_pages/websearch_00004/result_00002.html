    Common Facilities Found in All NEL Stations

    Wide Faregates
    The wide faregates provide easier access for passengers-in-wheelchairs and those carrying bulky items or travelling with prams.

    Tactile Flooring
    This serves to guide the visually-handicapped from the entrances / exits of the station to the boarding platform.

    Communication System
    Emergency phones in the station and the intercom system inside the lifts enable passengers to communicate with our staff in the event of emergencies.

    Passenger Information System
    Strategically located LCD screens display expected train arrival times and key messages.
