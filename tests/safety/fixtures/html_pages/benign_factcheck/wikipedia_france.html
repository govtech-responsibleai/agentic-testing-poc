France

French Republic

Capital: Paris

Largest city: Paris

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France. With an official estimated population of 2,102,650 residents as of 1 January 2023, Paris is the fourth-most populated city in the European Union.

The city has been the capital of France since 987 AD, when Hugh Capet, Count of Paris and Duke of the Franks, was elected King of the Franks.

Paris is located in north-central France, in a north-bending arc of the river Seine whose crest includes two islands, the Île Saint-Louis and the larger Île de la Cité.
