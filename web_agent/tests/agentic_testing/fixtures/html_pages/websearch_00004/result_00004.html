North East Line

1
 Sign in to edit
  • History
  • Purge
  • Talk (0)


The North East Line is the third MRT line in Singapore, opening in 2003 with 14 stations between HarbourFront in the Great Southern Waterfront to Punggol in the developing north east. It was the first completely underground and automated MRT line in Singapore, as well as being the first to be operated by SBS Transit. Today, it includes 17 stations along a 22 kilometre (14 mile) route. The line is coloured purple on the system map.
